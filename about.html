about us

Nestled in the heart of Nablus, "Hazem" is a beloved Palestinian restaurant renowned for its authentic flavors and warm hospitality. Established by Mahmoud Abu-Hazem decades ago as a humble falafel stand, it has grown into a culinary icon, offering a diverse menu of traditional dishes like musakhan and mansaf. Today, under Jamal Abu-Hazem's stewardship, "Hazem" remains a cherished gathering place where locals and visitors alike savor the rich flavors of Palestinian cuisine, celebrating heritage with every meal
